ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Brigham and I'm a recent graduate from the University of California, Berkeley where I got my Bachelors of Arts in Applied Mathematics with a Computer Science focus.

I am also starting my career as an international freelance photographer and filmmaker. Over the last few years, I have gained experience as a photographer and video producer. I have had the opportunity to shoot everything from major international events to personal portraits and headshots, honing my craft and deepening my love for photography every step of the way. Street photography has always been a passion of mine and I pride myself in my ability to capture culturally defining moments within the everyday atmosphere of the places I visit.

During my time in college I worked part time as a video producer for a local newspaper as well as a code coach for theCoderSchool Berkeley. Both of these experiences have allowed me to develop my skills and explore my love for digital media, combining a passion of creativity with my technical skills.
