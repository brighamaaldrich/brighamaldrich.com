WILDLIFE PHOTOGRAPHY

There are few things that I love over photography, but animals are one of them. I always take any chance I can get to watch and observe the behaviors of the wildlife around me without hesitation. Bringing a camera with me is just an excuse to do it more often. When I do decide to photograph my favorite animals I always shoot from the level of my subject so that I can get glimpse of what life looks like from their eyes.
